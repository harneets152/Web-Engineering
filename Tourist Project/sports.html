Intro to Sports

Sports teams in Los Angeles are prominent and are very well known. Los Angeles strives to go above and beyond which these teams do in their own respective sports. Los Angeles also seen as the city of Champions has had many titles awarded across a variety of sports. Additionally, the facilities are frequently being modified and retrofitted with the latest technology to enhance the spectator⁄fan experience.

Los Angeles is also known to be very competitive in certain sports which is why you’ll see more than one team for a specific sport. Additionally, you’ll notice that the market for sports in Los Angeles is sustainable in terms of capital and revenue generated for specific franchises for their own respective sports.

Basketball

Los Angeles Sparks

The Los Angeles Sparks are a professional basketball team that plays for the league called the WNBA, similar to the NBA. The WNBA is also known as the Women’s National Basketball Association was founded on April 22, 1996. The Team was founded before the League’s 1997 season began. This franchise is known for the most recent back–to–back win for the title. The lakers owner, Jerry Bus previously owned the franchise from 1997 to 2006. The Sparks were known as a sister team to the Los Angeles Lakers (An LA–based team that plays within the NBA). Notable players on the Sparks roster include 6–foot 4–inch center Candace Parker and point guard Nikki Teasley. Along with the rest of the roster, this team has managed to win back–to–back titles for the WNBA. The years that they won were 2001, 2002, 2003, 2016, and 2017.

Los Angeles Lakers

The Los Angeles Lakers are a professional basketball team that plays for the league called the NBA, similar to the WNBA. The NBA is also known as the National Basketball Association which was founded on June 6, 1946. The Team formed after buying a disbanded team which at the time were called the Detriot Gems and they played for the NBL which was known as the National Basketball League. At first, the team started to play out of Minneapolis, Minnesota and they were called the Minneapolis Lakers. Before the NBA, the team won one NBL Championship in 1948. This franchise is known for multiple appearances in the NBA Finals however they lost a majority of the early appearances to their rival team which was the Boston Celtics. They won their first championship in 1976 with hall of fame Wilt Chamberlain. Jerry Bus would later acquire the Lakers in a purchase around 1979. They went on to get many famous players such as Magic Johnson, Kareem Abdul–Jabbar, and James Worthy. Eventually, these players would retire and a new era of basketball would echo change for the whole league. Specifically, the draft of Kobe Bryant and center Shaquille O’Neal would be an unstoppable duo with the help of their head coach which was Phil Jackson. They won the championship three times in a row (three–peat) from 2000 to 2002 and managed to win in 2009 and 2010. The new team which is composed of returning veterans such as Lebron James and Trevor Ariza is mixed with somewhat new faces such as Anthony Davis and Russel Westbrook. They won the 2020 championship and commemorated that win to Kobe Bryant who passed away earlier that year.
“Everything negative – pressure, challenges – is all an opportunity for me to rise” – Kobe Bryant.

Los Angeles Clippers

The Los Angeles Clippers are a professional basketball team that plays for the league called the NBA, similar to the WNBA. The team was formed as the Buffalo Braves in 1970 and they were led by Hall of Famer Bob McAdoo. They reached the playoffs 3 times during the span of 8 seasons under the name of the Braves. However, due to internal conflict, they relocated from Buffalo to San Diego and were called the San Diego Clippers. The owner decided to relocate from San Diego to Los Angeles without letting the league know and this caused some issues. The team has struggled during the the first 27 seasons in Los Angeles and only made 4 playoff appearances to where they only won the first round. The overall dynamic had changed during 2010 with the introduction of Blake Griffin, Chris Paul, and DeAndre Jordan. They still struggled until 2021 when the team reached the Conference Finals but fell short. This team is the oldest franchise to never make a NBA Final’s appearance. Popular stars on their roster include Kawhi Leonard, Paul George, and Reggie Jackson.

Football

Los Angeles Rams

The Los Angeles Rams are a professional football team that plays for the league called the NFL, specifically the NFC West Division. The NFL also known as the National Football League was formed in 1920 as the American Professional Football Association (APFA) they changed their name in 1922. The team was founded in 1936 as the Cleveland Rams in Ohio. They won their first championship in 1945 and moved to Los Angeles the following year. They had played in the Los Angeles Memorial Coliseum till 1980 when they had relocated to another facility in Anaheim. In 1994 they decided to leave California and move to St. Loius, Missouri where they won the Superbowl five seasons later. Sometime later they had decided to move back to Los Angeles and they’ve recently won the Superbowl 56 against the Cincinnati Bengals. They’ve won three Superbowls in total (2 in Los Angeles and 1 in St. Louis).

Los Angeles Chargers

The Los Angeles Chargers are a professional football team that plays for the league called the NFL, specifically the AFC West Division. The team was founded in Los Angeles in 1959 and started to play in 1960 as part of the AFL. Their first season was in Los Angeles and had relocated the following year to San Diego to become the San Diego Chargers. They joined the NFL as part of the merger that took place between AFL and NFL in 1970. They won the AFL Championship in 1963 and reached the playoffs 5 times and the Championship game 4 times before the merger with the NFL. They”ve made 13 appearances in the playoffs since the merger and 4 appearances in the AFC Championship game. In 1994 they won their first and only championship title but ended up losing the Superbowl to the San Francisco 49ers.

Soccer

LA Galaxy

The Los Angeles Galaxy is also known as the LA Galaxy is a professional soccer club that plays for the MLS, specifically the Western Conference. The club was founded in Los Angeles in 1994 and started to play in 1996 by Anschutz Entertainment Group. This Franchise is described to be the most successful due to it having a record 5 MLS cups and 4 appearances to the finals. They’ve won the Western Conference regular–season title 8 times.

Baseball

Los Angeles Angels

The Los Angeles Angels are a professional baseball team that plays for the MLB, specifically the American League (AL) West Division. The MLB is also known as the Major League Baseball is a baseball organization and the oldest major professional sports league in the world. The National League and the American League were formed separately and merged in 2000. The Angels are the first team to form from California. They’ve played games at Anaheim in their facility which is the Angel Stadium. The team was founded in 1961 by Gene Autry. They played in Los Angeles until moving to Anaheim in 1966. The team went from being called the Californian Angels to the Anaheim Angels after their move. They changed to the Los Angeles Angels after Los Angeles was approved as a suitable replacement for Anaheim in 2005. They won the World Series in 2002 and it’s been their only championship in MLB.

Los Angeles Dodgers

The Los Angeles Dodgers are a professional baseball team that plays for the MLB, specifically the National League (AL) West Division. The team was made in 1883 in the city of Brooklyn and the team joined the NL in 1890 under a different name which was the Brooklyn Bridegrooms and went through many names until finally settling on the name of the Dodgers in 1932. Their rival team which was the New York Yankees formed between the 1940s through the 1950s as they faced each other 7 times in the World Series. The Dodgers lost the first five matches against the Yankees during their time in the Finals. Their first win happened in 1955 and they made history with Jackie Robinson’s debut which was in 1947 he was the first African American to play in the Major Leagues since 1884. Don Newcombe was another player to make history on the Dodgers as he was the first to win both the Cy Young Award and the NL MVP during the same season. After 68 seasons they relocated the team to Los Angeles and they had played their first four seasons in the Los Angeles Memorial Coliseum before moving to their Dodger Stadium in 1962. They won the World Series during their first year in Los Angeles and picked up two more World Series wins in the 1960s. In 1980 a Mexican pitcher named Fernando Valenzuela who got the name “Fernandomania” became the first player to ever win the Cy Young and Rookie of the Year award in the same season. They went on to win another World Series in 1988 and then had a long drought period up until 2013 to 2020 where the Dodgers won the Division title back to back and managed to snag the World Series in 2020. They are the most successful and popular MLB teams to date and they maintain their rivalry with the San Francisco Giants.
